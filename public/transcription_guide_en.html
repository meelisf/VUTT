Hyphenation

Typeface Symbol Example
Antiqua - (standard hyphen) coa-
cervare
Fractur / Gothic ⸗ (double hyphen) Ge⸗
witter

Ligatures

  • æ, Æ, œ, Œ – transcribe using the specific character
  • ﬆ, ﬀ, ﬁ, ﬂ etc. – expand (st, ff, fi, fl)

Umlauts and Accents

  • ö, ä, ü, õ – always use modern forms
  • uͤ, oͤ, aͤ (letter + small e above) – transcribe as ü, ö, ä
  • å, Å (Swedish) – keep as is
  • ũ, ñ, õ – keep as is (tilde is preserved)

Special Characters

  • ſ (long s) – transcribe as ſ
  • ß (double s) – transcribe as ß

Abbreviations

  • que abbreviation (ꝗ etc.) – write as q;
  • -us abbreviation (ꝰ) – can be expanded
